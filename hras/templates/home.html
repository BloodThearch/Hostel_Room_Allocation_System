    Hostel room allocation system is designed for students of Thapar Institute to allocate their rooms with ease. New users will be required to create new accounts with the following information like their name, age, gender, roll number, CGPA, Thapar.edu accounts, etc. the staff will be required to enter range of the CGPA students allowed for the session along with start and end time of the session. Then students will be notified about their sessions when their respective room allotment will start. During that session, students are highly advised to select their rooms. After completing the purchase, the room will be permanently locked till the end of the semester.
